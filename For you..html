I’ve been thinking about this,

I don’t really know how to say this perfectly… only that I yearn for you.

It’s the way I find myself wanting you around, wanting to share more, build more, feel more with you.

For someone like me, drowning was never supposed to be an option. But if I’m going under, let it be in your smile.

I found something in you that feels like home before I even knew I was lost.

Maybe it comes out messy. But every version of me is here, trying to get closer to you.

So when I say I yearn for you, I mean I’ve already chosen you in ways you haven’t even noticed yet.

I want something real with you, not just “seeing how it goes,” but choosing each other properly.

Will you be my girlfriend?

Yay!

This is the start of something real.

I’ll treat this ‘yes’ like it matters because you do.
